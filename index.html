Hello, I am

Moses Chimega





I am a web developer based in Mombasa,Kenya.I was born in Likoni Mombasa where i started my primary education at Bethania Academy and completed my secondary education at Sacred Heart Boys High School in Rongai,Nakuru.I further studied Computer Science and graduated at the University Of Nairobi.I currently help people who desire to have a passion in art so that they can achieve their goals and also assist others.



I love art because i dont think about making art but i just get it done.I let everyone else decide if it's good or bad, whether they love it or hate it. While they are deciding, I make even more art.Art is God's way of saying hello. So pay attention to poetry. Pay attention to music. Pay attention to paintings and sculptures and photo exhibits and ballets and plays. Don't let all this go unnoticed. Your world is shouting out to you, revealing something intrinsically glorious about itself. Listen carefully. Love art, the way art loves life.
